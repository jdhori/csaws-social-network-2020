About this edition

Each chart is presented as an interactive graphic with a static-image fallback and an always-present data table, so the data is reachable by keyboard, screen reader, and in print. No figure was altered, dropped, or invented: the two page-2 bar charts carry no numbers in the source, so they are shown as ordered categories with relative magnitude and an explicit “no exact percentages” note. A 988 crisis resource is included and clearly marked as an editorial addition, not part of the original. The full accessibility audit documents every conformance decision, and the rights notice records the accessible-format basis and third-party tooling terms.
